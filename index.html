About Me

I am a second-year master's student at Nanjing University, currently interning at Microsoft Research. I am now focusing on scalable post-training techniques for multimodal models.

Previously, I interned at OPPO Research and Microsoft Research Asia. I received my B.Eng. degree from Xidian University, and spent a wonderful time with my friends in Xi'an, China from 2020 to 2024.

I maintain a blog (in Chinese), where I write about my life and learning experience.

Feel free to reach out via email or wechat.
